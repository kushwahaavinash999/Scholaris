Samsung Star Scholarship

About


  • Samsung Star Scholar 2021-22 is an initiative for students of Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya (JNVs) currently pursuing a full-term B.E./B.Tech./Dual Degree (B.Tech.+ M.Tech.) course in an Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) or a National Institute of Technology (NIT) in India. This scholarship aims to award meritorious students pursuing technical courses at top institutions. The selected scholars will receive a scholarship of up to INR 2 lakh for expenses related to tuition, examination, hostel and mess, for one academic year.

Eligibility


  • Be enrolled in B.E./B.Tech./Dual degree (B.Tech.+M.Tech.) programme in IITs and NITs listed by Samsung
  • Have completed Class 12 from any of the Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya (JNVs)
  • For detailed information, kindly refer to the 'Rule Book'.

Document Required / Selection Criteria


  • Supporting receipts/documents (signed and stamped) and verified by the Institute’s Dean Academic or Institute’s designated authority
  • Educational certificates
  • First-year scholarship candidate(s) will be selected on the basis of their All India Rank (AIR) in the Joint Entrance Examination (Main).
  • The 2nd year scholarship will be determined based on the students’ overall academic performance.
  • The candidates who remain in the Top 50% of their class will continue to get the scholarship for the next year.
  • For detailed information, kindly refer to the 'Rule Book'.

How to Apply


  • Fresh Candidates : Click on the 'Apply Now' button below and read all details.
  • Download the application form
  • Send a scanned copy of the application form, attach the relevant documents through email at star.scholar@samsung.com.
  • Also, send the hard copy of the application by post to the address below:
  • STAR SCHOLAR
    Corporate Citizenship (CRO)
    Samsung India Electronics Pvt. Ltd.,
    20th ~ 24th Floor, Two Horizon Centre, Golf Course Road,
    Sector - 43, DLF Phase V, Gurgaon, Haryana -122002, India
  • Renewal Candidates : Candidates who are already enrolled in any school / college and had applied for scholarships last year will renew their application.
  • For More Information Must Read the Full Instruction.
  • Aadhar Card is Compulsory for the Scholarship Application 2020-21

Terms and Condation


  • Only applications that have been verified by the Dean Academics or designated authority of the Institute will be accepted and considered eligible.
  • Clear and legible copies of all supporting documents as mentioned in the application form like Fees receipts, fees structure, academic documents etc. should be submitted as proofs
  • Duration of the scholarship will be for one (1) academic year, extendable to subsequent three and Four (3/4) years for B.E./B. Tech. degree and Dual Degree courses respectively based on the fulfilment of eligibility criteria and selection approval by Samsung.
  • The applicants must comply with all applicable enrolment and registration deadlines as published.
  • The scholarships can not be sold, transferred or assigned and are not convertible to cash.
  • Samsung will award scholarships at its sole discretion. All decisions of Samsung will be binding and final.
  • Any other courses like B.ARCH/BSC/MSC etc. are not covered under this Program.
  • The applicants who have not completed Eighteen years (18) of age at the time of application must have their parent(s)’/legal guardian(s)’, countersign the application form. In the event that there is a constraint of meeting the submission deadline, parent(s)/legal guardian(s) will submit a declaration in original and in the format, as prescribed by Samsung, within 10 days of the submitted form. In case, the applicant fails to submit the declaration by the parent/legal guardian, Samsung reserves the right to cancel the application of the applicant.
