Australia Awards

About


  • It is a merit-based scholarship provided by the Department of Education, Government of Australia available for both local and international tertiary education providers. A scholarship of AUD 15,000 and AUD 1500 is given to qualified candidates for one academic year. For next year, candidates have to renew the scholarship based on their academic performance. It covers tuition fees and living expenses of students.

Eligibility


  • Minimum 75% in the previous level of education
  • Must be a tertiary education provider.
  • The student applying for the scholarship must commence a new study program in the next year after receiving the grant.
  • The student must have accepted full-time course in a regional campus and remains enrolled for the duration of grant.
  • International student must have accepted a full-time study course registered on CRICOS.
  • Students must maintain an ongoing residence in a regional area for the duration of grant.
  • Have a valid Australian student visa.

Document Required


  • Passport (ordinary and valid)
  • Identification documents (PAN Card/Voter Card/Aadhaar Card)
  • Birth certificate
  • Degree certificates (Bachelors and any Post Graduate level), or certified academic transcript if degree is yet to be conferred. Transcripts must show results for each semester as well as overall results, and must clearly show the GPA. Where transcripts are not in English, please provide an English translation.
  • Certificates of special achievements or merit which are relevant to the proposed study program
  • IELTS or TOEFL or PTE certificate

How to Apply


    There is no single or common application process for Destination Australia Scholarship Program. Since applicants need to apply for the scholarship via 35 eligible education providers , it is up to them how they want to distribute these scholarships.
    The step-by-step application process for such universities is mentioned below:

  • Submit an application for an eligible course of study.
  • Write and submit a one-page statement demonstrating “How Destination Australia Scholarship will assist you in reaching your career goals and how it will help you make tomorrow better.”
  • The assessment panel will evaluate your application and SOP for the scholarships.
  • Outcome notifications will be sent via university to successful candidates. All unsuccessful candidates will also be notified.
  • Selected students will have to confirm their acceptance of Destination Australia Scholarship within the specified time and via a specified mode of confirmation like email.
    • Note: students must check in with respective Universities/institutes for Destination Australia Scholarship application deadlines before applying.
