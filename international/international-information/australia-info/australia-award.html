Australia Awards

About


  • Australia awards is a government funded scholarship long-term program offering aid to international students for full-time undergraduate and postgraduate study through the minimum time required to complete the study. It provides full tuition fee, return air travel (single return is paid for), establishment allowance, contribution to living expenses, overseas student health cover, pre-course English fees, supplementary academic support and other benefits. All recipients of this scholarship inevitably become a part of Australia Global Alumni.

Eligibility


  • General eligibility criteria includes knowledge of English, ability to face challenges in Australia on the educational front, maturity, resilience and health well suited to Australian conditions.
  • Above or of the age 18 on 1 February of year when scholarship commences.
  • Be a citizen of participating country and not be a permanent resident of Australia.
  • Not be married to or engaged to any person who holds permanent residency of Australia or New Zealand.
  • Satisfy admission criteria of the institution they apply to.
  • Not be current serving military personnel.
  • Be a recent graduate from one of the target institutions or be in the final year of a tertiary level qualification from a target institution equivalent to Australiaâ€™s bachelor degree.
  • Have minimum cumulative grade point equivalent of 7.5
  • Have an endorsement letter from their university in India.

Document Required


  • Passport (ordinary and valid)
  • Identification documents (PAN Card/Voter Card/Aadhaar Card)
  • Birth certificate
  • Degree certificates (Bachelors and any Post Graduate level), or certified academic transcript if degree is yet to be conferred. Transcripts must show results for each semester as well as overall results, and must clearly show the GPA. Where transcripts are not in English, please provide an English translation.
  • Certificates of special achievements or merit which are relevant to the proposed study program
  • IELTS or TOEFL or PTE certificate

How to Apply


  • All applications must be lodged online through OASIS at https://oasis.dfat.gov.au/ .
  • After selection, an interview round is done and then the candidates are selected.
