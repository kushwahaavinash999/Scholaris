Australian Government Research and Training Program (AGRTP)

About


  • It is a government offered centrally managed scholarship and provides financial aid to students for tuition fees and health insurance for up to three years. It is available for both domestic and international students. It supports a student’s Research Doctorate and Research Master’s degree. It also offers thesis allowance and dependent child allowance (for international students only).

Eligibility


  • Must have completed a degree equivalent to an Australian first class hons degree.
  • Must have a proof of English proficiency.
  • Must satisfy the admission requirements of the university a student is applying to.
  • Must enroll as a full-time student at a study or research program.
  • Must not be receiving any other commonwealth funded postgraduate financial aid.

Document Required


  • Academic transcripts
  • Evidence of publications, output in the arts or music, professional experience
  • English language requirements:-Please note that results of English Language Proficiency Tests including IELTS, TOEFL and PTE are not required to assess your scholarship application. You may need to submit these documents to the Admission Office for your admission application.
  • Scanned documents:-The University accepts scanned documents submitted online in support of applications for admission to courses of study and photocopy of documents for RTP applications.

How to Apply


  • No application is required for this scholarship.
  • All candidates who are eligible are considered for the grant.
  • Select the appropriate options in admission applications to be considered for available scholarships.
  • Application deadlines vary with university.
  • International students have to apply for admission by August
