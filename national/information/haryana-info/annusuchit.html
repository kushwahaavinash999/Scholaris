Annusuchit Jati Chhattra Ucch Shiksha protsahan Yojana

About


  • Annusuchit Jati Chhattra Ucch Shiksha protsahan Yojana, Haryana is an initiative for the students who have passed class 12 to enable them to provide financial assistance to SC Students Level and belongs to the SC Students. The selected students will receive variable awards.

Eligibility


  • The applicant should be have pursuing studies in diploma, graduation or postgraduation level degree in Commerce, Science or other.
  • Scholarship for SC Student category .
  • The annual income of the family should be between INR 1 Lakh and INR 2.40 Lakh from all sources.
  • They must have at least 60% attendance in the class.
  • For SC Students.

Document Required


  • Domicile Certificate
  • A Passport size photograph
  • caste certificate
  • Income declaration, a certificate from the parents/guardians
  • Mark sheet of previously qualified examination

How to Apply


  • Fresh Candidates : Whoever is a new candidate who has taken addmission this year should be studies in diploma, graduation or postgraduation level degree in Commerce, Science or other should apply in fresh.
  • Renewal Candidates : Candidates who are already enrolled in any school / college and had applied for scholarships last year will renew their application.
  • Kindly Collect the All Document Before Apply Online
  • If you are not now of the non refundable Amount, please check your fees receipt OR contact the college / school.
  • Submit your form to the concerned school / college within 3 days of the completion of the application.
  • For More Information Must Read the Full Instruction.
  • Aadhar Card is Compulsory for the Scholarship Application
  • Apply offline by collecting the form from TWO/DWO and submit the completed application to the concerned Head of the Institution.
