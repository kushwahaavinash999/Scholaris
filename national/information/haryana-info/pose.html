Promotion of Science Education (POSE) Scholarship Scheme

About


  • Promotion of Science Education (POSE) Scholarship Scheme, Haryana is an initiative for the students who have passed class 10 and 12 and applying for BSC or integrated MSC/MS program in natural and basic science to enable them to provide financial assistance to all Students Level and belongs to the all Students. The selected students will receive variable awards.

Eligibility


  • The applicant should be have passed class 10 and 12 board examination from a school in Haryana.
  • with minimum 85%marks (for Haryana Board school) and 90% marks (for other board such as CBSE/ICSE)in class 12.
  • Scholarship for students applying for a 3-year BSC/4-year BS or integrated MSC/MS program in natural and basic sciences .
  • The annual income of the family should be between INR 1 Lakh and INR 2.40 Lakh from all sources.
  • The students applying for a 2-year MSC program in natural and basic science can also apply provided they have passed class 10,12 and BSC/BA from a school/University/College in Haryana with minimum 75% marks in BSC/BA degree.
  • They must have at least 60% attendance in the class.
  • For all Students.

Document Required


  • Domicile Certificate
  • A Passport size photograph
  • caste certificate
  • Income declaration, a certificate from the parents/guardians
  • Mark sheet of previously qualified examination

How to Apply


  • Fresh Candidates : Whoever is a new candidate who has taken addmission this year should be studies in diploma, graduation or postgraduation level degree in Commerce, Science or other should apply in fresh.
  • Renewal Candidates : Candidates who are already enrolled in any school / college and had applied for scholarships last year will renew their application.
  • Kindly Collect the All Document Before Apply Online
  • If you are not now of the non refundable Amount, please check your fees receipt OR contact the college / school.
  • Submit your form to the concerned school / college within 3 days of the completion of the application.
  • For More Information Must Read the Full Instruction.
  • Aadhar Card is Compulsory for the Scholarship Application
