Post-matric scholarships for SC/ST/OBC

About


  • Post-matric scholarships for SC/ST/OBC,Tripura is an initiative for the students who are pursuing studies at the class 11 to PG level to enable them to provide financial assistance to SC/ST/OBC category Students Level and belongs to the SC/ST/OBC category Students. The selected students will receive variable awards.

Eligibility


  • The scholarship is open who are pursuing studies at the class 11 to PG level.
  • Scholarship for SC/ST/OBC community students .
  • The annual family income of the applicant should be less than INR 2 Lakh (for ST), INR 1.5 Lakh(for OBC) and INR 2.5 Lakh (for SC students).
  • The students also need to have an attendance of 75% or above at the end of each quarter.

Document Required


  • Domicile Certificate
  • A Passport size photograph
  • caste certificate
  • Income declaration, a certificate from the parents/guardians
  • Mark sheet of previously qualified examination

How to Apply


  • Fresh Candidates : Whoever is a new candidate who has taken addmission this year The students who are pursuing studies at the class 11 to PG level level should apply in fresh.
  • Renewal Candidates : Candidates who are already enrolled in any school / college and had applied for scholarships last year will renew their application.
  • Kindly Collect the All Document Before Apply Online
  • If you are not now of the non refundable Amount, please check your fees receipt OR contact the college / school.
  • Submit your form to the concerned school / college within 3 days of the completion of the application.
  • For More Information Must Read the Full Instruction.
  • Aadhar Card is Compulsory for the Scholarship Application
