Post Matric Intermediate Scholarship for SC,ST,OBC Students

About


  • Postmatric Intermediate Scholarship for ST,SC,OBC Students, Andhra Pradesh is an initiative for the students who are studying in Post-Matriculation Level i.e. class 11th to PG Level to enable them to provide financial assistance to ST,SC,OBC Students. Level and belongs to the ST,SC,OBC category. The selected students will receive variable awards.

Eligibility


  • The students should also have an attendance of 75% at the end of each quarter
  • Studying in Post-Matriculation Level i.e. class 11th to PG Level
  • Belong to ST,SC,OBC & Disabled welfare category
  • Have a family income of not more than INR 2 lakh per annum

Document Required


  • Domicile Certificate
  • A Passport size photograph
  • Income declaration, a certificate from the parents/guardians
  • Mark sheet of previously qualified examination

How to Apply


  • Fresh Candidates : Whoever is a new candidate who has taken addmission this year should be in Post-Matriculation Level i.e. class 11th to PG Level. should apply in fresh.
  • Renewal Candidates : Candidates who are already enrolled in any school / college and had applied for scholarships last year will renew their application.
  • Kindly Collect the All Document Before Apply Online
  • If you are not now of the non refundable Amount, please check your fees receipt OR contact the college / school.
  • Submit your form to the concerned school / college within 3 days of the completion of the application.
  • For More Information Must Read the Full Instruction.
  • Aadhar Card is Compulsory for the Scholarship Application
