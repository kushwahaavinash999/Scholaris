Scholarship to ST students for pursuing Higher Studies

About


  • Scholarship to ST Students Pursuing Higher Studies ,Andaman-and-Nicobar is an initiative for the students who are Pursuing Higher Studies to enable them to provide financial assistance to Students.The selected students will receive variable awards.

Eligibility


  • Domicile of Andaman & Nicobar
  • Must pass Secondary (class X)/ Senior Secondary (class 12th) from a recognized board of school education
  • Must be pursuing any of the following courses: Degree of Graduation & Post Graduation.phil, Ph.D.,
  • Post-Doctoral Programmes Post-Doctoral Research in Medicine(Allopathy,Indian & other recognized systems of medicines),Engineering, Technology, Planning, Architecture, Design, Fashion Design and Technology, Agriculture, Veterinary & Allied sciences, Management, Business Finance/Administration,Computer science/Applications.
  • Post-Graduate Diploma courses in Various branches of management & Medicine, Commercial Pilot License C.A/I.C.W.A/C.S./I.C.F.A/etc.
  • Graduate/Post-Graduate leading to Degree,Diploma,Certificate in areas like Pharmacy (B.Pharm),,nursing(B.Nursing),LLB,BFS,other Para-Medical branches like Rehabilitation Diagnostics, etc., Mass Communication ,Hotel Management & catering, Travel/ Tourism/ Hospitality Management etc Post Matriculation Level non- degree courses
  • Belong to Scheduled Tribes
  • Have a family income of not more than INR 2 lakh per annum

Document Required


  • Domicile Certificate
  • A Passport size photograph
  • Schedule Tribe Certificate duly signed by an authorised Revenue Officer
  • Income declaration, a certificate from the parents/guardians
  • Mark sheet of previously qualified examination
  • First page of Bank PassBook
  • Photo of Student
  • Signature of student

How to Apply


  • Fresh Candidates : Whoever is a new candidate who has taken addmission this year should be studying at Graduate/Post Graduate/Technical/Non-Technical. should apply in fresh.
  • Renewal Candidates : Candidates who are already enrolled in any school / college and had applied for scholarships last year will renew their application.
  • Kindly Collect the All Document Before Apply Online
  • If you are not now of the non refundable Amount, please check your fees receipt OR contact the college / school.
  • Submit your form to the concerned school / college within 3 days of the completion of the application.
  • For More Information Must Read the Full Instruction.
  • Aadhar Card is Compulsory for the Scholarship Application
