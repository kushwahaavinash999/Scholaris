Ambedkar Fellowship Scheme for SC Students

About


  • Ambedkar Fellowship Scheme for SC Students,Rajasthan is an initiative for the students who are pursuing admission to a PhD level to enable them to provide financial assistance to SC category Students Level and belongs to the SC category Students. The selected students will receive variable awards.

Eligibility


  • The scholarship is open who are pursuing admission to a PhD level from a private/government university overseas in the streams prescribed (Social Science, Public Administration, Law, Economics, Political Science, Anthropology).
  • The student must have completed their PG studies from a recognised university with minimum 55% marks.
  • Scholarship for SC community students .
  • The age of the applicant should be less than 35 Years.
  • The annual Income of the family should not be more than INR 2.50 Lakh.

Document Required


  • Domicile Certificate
  • A Passport size photograph
  • caste certificate
  • Income declaration, a certificate from the parents/guardians
  • Mark sheet of previously qualified examination

How to Apply


  • Fresh Candidates : Whoever is a new candidate who has taken addmission this year The students who are pursuing admission at the PhD level should apply in fresh.
  • Renewal Candidates : Candidates who are already enrolled in any school / college and had applied for scholarships last year will renew their application.
  • Kindly Collect the All Document Before Apply Online
  • If you are not now of the non refundable Amount, please check your fees receipt OR contact the college / school.
  • Submit your form to the concerned school / college within 3 days of the completion of the application.
  • For More Information Must Read the Full Instruction.
  • Aadhar Card is Compulsory for the Scholarship Application
  • Apply offline through the Secretary,Ambedkar Peeth, Mundla, Jamvaramgadh, Jaipur.
