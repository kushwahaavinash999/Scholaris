Pre-Matric Scholarship for SC/OBC/ST/EBC

About


  • Pre-Matric Scholarship for SC/OBC/ST/EBC,Puducherry is an initiative for the students who are pursuing studies in class 1 to 10 level to enable them to provide financial assistance to SC/OBC/ST/EBC category Students Level and belongs to the SC/OBC/ST/EBC category Students. The selected students will receive variable awards.

Eligibility


  • The scholarship is open who are pursuing studies in class 1 to 10 level from a private/government school/college.
  • SC/ST:-
    SC/ST Students (Class IX & X).
    The applicant student should belong to the SC/ST.
    The applicant student Parent/Guardian's income should not exceed Rs.2.50 lakh per annum.
    The applicant student will not get any other centrally-funded Pre Matric Scholarship.
  • OBC:-
    OBC Students (Class I to X).
    The student who is studying at the post-matriculation level (class 1 to 10) can apply for this scholarship.
    The student whose family Annual Income should not exceed INR 2.5 Lakh for OBC candidates also can apply for this scholarship.
  • EBC:-
    Economically Backward Class Students of VI to X.
    The applicant students must be resident of the Union Territory.
    The applicant must be a student of VI standard to X standard and studying in recognized institutions situated within the U.T. of Pondicherry.
    The concessions under the scheme can not be held conjointly with any other scholarships provided partly or wholly at the cost of the state or of the Government of India.

Document Required


  • Domicile Certificate
  • A Passport size photograph
  • caste certificate
  • Income declaration, a certificate from the parents/guardians
  • Mark sheet of previously qualified examination

How to Apply


  • Fresh Candidates : Whoever is a new candidate who has taken addmission this year The students who are studies in class 1 to 10 level should apply in fresh.
  • Renewal Candidates : Candidates who are already enrolled in any school / college and had applied for scholarships last year will renew their application.
  • Kindly Collect the All Document Before Apply Online
  • If you are not now of the non refundable Amount, please check your fees receipt OR contact the college / school.
  • Submit your form to the concerned school / college within 3 days of the completion of the application.
  • For More Information Must Read the Full Instruction.
  • Aadhar Card is Compulsory for the Scholarship Application
