Post Matric Intermediate Scholarship for OBC Students

About


  • Postmatric Intermediate Scholarship for OBC Students, Uttar Pradesh is an initiative for the students who are studying in Class 11 and 12 and belongs to the OBC category. The selected students will receive variable awards.

Eligibility


  • Domicile of Uttar Pradesh
  • Studying in Class 11 and 12
  • Belong to OBC Category
  • Have a family income of not more than INR 2 lakh per annum

Document Required


  • Residential proof (electricity bill, connection and gas bill, ration card, voter ID, Aadhaar card, pan card, or driving license).
  • Caste certificate for caste validity
  • Income Certificate
  • Domicile Certificate
  • Aadhar card Number
  • Bankpass Book
  • Current year fee receipt/admission letter
  • Student ID proof
  • Latest Passport Size Scan Photo
  • Mark sheet of previously qualified examination

How to Apply


  • Fresh Candidates : Whoever is a new candidate who has taken addmission this year should be in class 11 , 12 (Intermidiate) course, should apply in fresh.
  • Renewal Candidates : Candidates who are already enrolled in any school / college and had applied for scholarships last year will renew their application.
  • Kindly Collect the All Document Before Apply Online
  • If you are not now of the non refundable Amount, please check your fees receipt OR contact the college / school.
  • Submit your form to the concerned school / college within 3 days of the completion of the application.
  • For More Information Must Read the Full Instruction.
  • Aadhar Card is Compulsory for the Scholarship Application 2020-21
