Pre Matric Scholarship for Minorities

About


  • Pre-Matric Scholarship for Minority Students, Uttar Pradesh is an initiative for students of Class 9 and 10 who belong to the minority communities of Uttar Pradesh. The scholarship aims to provide financial assistance to needy students in completing their studies at the matriculation level.

Eligibility


  • Domicile of Uttar Pradesh
  • Studying in Class 9 and 10
  • Belong to the Minority community
  • Have a family income of not more than INR 1 lakh per annum

Document Required


  • Self-declaration of minority community certificate by the student
  • Residential proof (electricity bill, connection and gas bill, ration card, voter ID, Aadhaar card, pan card, or driving license).
  • Caste Certificate (if required)
  • Income Certificate
  • Domicile Certificate
  • Aadhar card Number
  • Bankpass Book
  • Current year fee receipt/admission letter
  • Annual Non Refundable Amount
  • Latest Passport Size Scan Photo
  • Mark sheet of previously qualified examination

How to Apply


  • Fresh Candidates : Whoever is a new candidate who has taken addmission this year should be in class 9 ,10 (High School) course, should apply in fresh.
  • Renewal Candidates : Candidates who are already enrolled in any school / college and had applied for scholarships last year will renew their application.
  • Kindly Collect the All Document Before Apply Online
  • If you are not now of the non refundable Amount, please check your fees receipt OR contact the college / school.
  • Submit your form to the concerned school / college within 3 days of the completion of the application.
  • For More Information Must Read the Full Instruction.
  • Aadhar Card is Compulsory for the Scholarship Application 2020-21
