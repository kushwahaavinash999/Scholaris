Post Graduate Merit Scholarship (Medhabruti)

About


  • Post Graduate Merit Scholarship (Medhabruti),Odisha is an initiative for the students who are pursuing a Post-Graduate to enable them to provide financial assistance to all category Students Level and belongs to the all category Students. The selected students will receive variable awards.

Eligibility


  • The scholarship is open who are pursuing a Post Graduate degree from any recognized institution/university.
  • The student shall apply for the Medhabruti only in the year of joining (1st year of the course).
  • Scholarship for all community students .
  • The student must have scored 60% marks in their Graduate degree passed a +3 degree.
  • The annual family income of the student from all sources shall not exceed INR 6 Lakh per annum.

Document Required


  • Domicile Certificate
  • A Passport size photograph
  • caste certificate
  • Income declaration, a certificate from the parents/guardians
  • Mark sheet of previously qualified examination

How to Apply


  • Fresh Candidates : Whoever is a new candidate who has taken addmission this year The students who are pursuing a Post-Graduate degree should apply in fresh.
  • Renewal Candidates : Candidates who are already enrolled in any school / college and had applied for scholarships last year will renew their application.
  • Kindly Collect the All Document Before Apply Online
  • If you are not now of the non refundable Amount, please check your fees receipt OR contact the college / school.
  • Submit your form to the concerned school / college within 3 days of the completion of the application.
  • For More Information Must Read the Full Instruction.
  • Aadhar Card is Compulsory for the Scholarship Application
