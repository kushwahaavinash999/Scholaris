Mukhyamantri Yuva Swavalamban Yojana

About


  • Mukhyamantri Yuva Swavalamban Yojana for Students for those whose have passed class 11, 12, and getting admission in first year graduation, Gujarat is an initiative for the students who are studying in their regular studies have passed class 11, 12, and getting admission in first year graduation, first year graduation to enable them to provide financial assistance to all community Students. Level and belongs to the all category. The selected students will receive variable awards.

Eligibility


  • The applicant should be have passed class 11, 12, and getting admission in first year graduation.
  • Belong to all Community.
  • Students must have scored at least 80% percentile in class 10th/12th.
  • Students must have scored at least 65% percentile in diploma.
  • The annual income of the family should not be more than INR 6 Lakh.

Document Required


  • Domicile Certificate
  • A Passport size photograph
  • caste certificate
  • Income declaration, a certificate from the parents/guardians
  • Mark sheet of previously qualified examination

How to Apply


  • Fresh Candidates : Whoever is a new candidate who has taken addmission this year should be have passed class 11, 12, and getting admission in first year graduation. should apply in fresh.
  • Renewal Candidates : Candidates who are already enrolled in any school / college and had applied for scholarships last year will renew their application.
  • Kindly Collect the All Document Before Apply Online
  • If you are not now of the non refundable Amount, please check your fees receipt OR contact the college / school.
  • Submit your form to the concerned school / college within 3 days of the completion of the application.
  • For More Information Must Read the Full Instruction.
  • Aadhar Card is Compulsory for the Scholarship Application
