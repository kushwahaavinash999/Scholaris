Combined Counselling Board (CCB)

About


  • Combined Counselling Board (CCB),Jammu & Kashmir is an initiative for the students who are pursuing studies in Diploma,UG & PG Level courses to enable them to provide financial assistance to all category Students Level and belongs to the all category Students. The selected students will receive variable awards.

Eligibility


  • The students should be studies in Diploma,UG & PG Level courses.
  • Scholarship for all Student category .
  • The annual income of the student family should be less than INR 2 Lakh.
  • The student must have passed the last qualifying examination with at least 40% to 50% marks.

Document Required


  • Domicile Certificate
  • A Passport size photograph
  • caste certificate
  • Income declaration, a certificate from the parents/guardians
  • Mark sheet of previously qualified examination

How to Apply


  • Fresh Candidates : Whoever is a new candidate who has taken addmission this year The students should be studies in Diploma,UG & PG Level courses should apply in fresh.
  • Renewal Candidates : Candidates who are already enrolled in any school / college and had applied for scholarships last year will renew their application.
  • Kindly Collect the All Document Before Apply Online
  • If you are not now of the non refundable Amount, please check your fees receipt OR contact the college / school.
  • Submit your form to the concerned school / college within 3 days of the completion of the application.
  • For More Information Must Read the Full Instruction.
  • Aadhar Card is Compulsory for the Scholarship Application
