Attendance scholarship to Girls Students

About


  • Attendance scholarship to Girls Students for those whose studying in class 1 to 5 in non-model government school,Chandigarh is an initiative for the students who are must passed 12 in any streams to enable them to provide financial assistance to Girls Students. Level and belongs to the Girls category. The selected students will receive variable awards.

Eligibility


  • studying in class 1 to 5 in non-model government school.
  • The candidates must be permanent resident of chandigarh
  • Belong to Girls students.
  • The annual income of the family should not more than INR 1.50 Lakh.
  • They must not be in receipt of any other scholarship.
  • candidates should have scored at least 50% marks in the last qualifying examination.
  • The student must have achieved a monthly attendance of 75% or more.

Document Required


  • Domicile Certificate
  • A Passport size photograph
  • caste certificate
  • Income declaration, a certificate from the parents/guardians
  • Mark sheet of previously qualified examination

How to Apply


  • Fresh Candidates : Whoever is a new candidate who has taken addmission this year should be studying in class 1 to 5 in non-model government school. should apply in fresh.
  • Renewal Candidates : Candidates who are already enrolled in any school / college and had applied for scholarships last year will renew their application.
  • Kindly Collect the All Document Before Apply Online
  • If you are not now of the non refundable Amount, please check your fees receipt OR contact the college / school.
  • Submit your form to the concerned school / college within 3 days of the completion of the application.
  • For More Information Must Read the Full Instruction.
  • Aadhar Card is Compulsory for the Scholarship Application
