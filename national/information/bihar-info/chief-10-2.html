Chief minister Kanya Utthan Yojana for 10+2

About


  • Chief minister Kanya Utthan Yojana for 10+2 for those whose passed 12 examination,Bihar is an initiative for the students who are studying in their regular studies post-matriculation levelto enable them to provide financial assistance to only girls.. The selected students will receive variable awards.

Eligibility


  • The unmarried girl students of Bihar who have passed their class 12 examination from Bihae school Examination Board can apply.
  • Only girls.

Document Required


  • Domicile Certificate
  • A Passport size photograph
  • caste certificate
  • Income declaration, a certificate from the parents/guardians
  • Mark sheet of previously qualified examination

How to Apply


  • Fresh Candidates : Whoever is a new candidate who has taken addmission this year should passed 12 examination. should apply in fresh.
  • Renewal Candidates : Candidates who are already enrolled in any school / college and had applied for scholarships last year will renew their application.
  • Kindly Collect the All Document Before Apply Online
  • If you are not now of the non refundable Amount, please check your fees receipt OR contact the college / school.
  • Submit your form to the concerned school / college within 3 days of the completion of the application.
  • For More Information Must Read the Full Instruction.
  • Aadhar Card is Compulsory for the Scholarship Application
