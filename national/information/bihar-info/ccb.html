Combined Counselling Board (CCB) Scholarship

About


  • Combined Counselling Board (CCB) Scholarship for those whose pursuing Degree or Postgraduate level courses,Bihar is an initiative for the students who are studying in their regular studies Degree or Postgraduate level courses to enable them to provide financial assistance to all community Students. Level and belongs to the all category. The selected students will receive variable awards.

Eligibility


  • The students studying at the college/university level (pursuing diploma level, degree level or postgraduate level courses) can apply.
  • Belong to all Community.
  • The annual income of the family should not more than INR 2.50 Lakh.
  • They must not be in receipt of any other scholarship.
  • candidates should have scored at least 40%-50% marks in the last qualifying examination.

Document Required


  • Domicile Certificate
  • A Passport size photograph
  • caste certificate
  • Income declaration, a certificate from the parents/guardians
  • Mark sheet of previously qualified examination

How to Apply


  • Fresh Candidates : Whoever is a new candidate who has taken addmission this year should be in Degree or Postgraduate level courses. should apply in fresh.
  • Renewal Candidates : Candidates who are already enrolled in any school / college and had applied for scholarships last year will renew their application.
  • Kindly Collect the All Document Before Apply Online
  • If you are not now of the non refundable Amount, please check your fees receipt OR contact the college / school.
  • Submit your form to the concerned school / college within 3 days of the completion of the application.
  • For More Information Must Read the Full Instruction.
  • Aadhar Card is Compulsory for the Scholarship Application
