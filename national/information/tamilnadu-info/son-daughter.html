Scholarship to Son and Daughter of Differently Abled Persons

About


  • Scholarship to Son and Daughter of Differently Abled Persons,Tamil Nadu is an initiative for the students who are pursuing studies in class 11 to PG level to enable them to provide financial assistance to all category Students Level and belongs to the all category Students. The selected students will receive variable awards.

Eligibility


  • The scholarship is open who are pursuing studies in class 11 to PG level(including ITI/Polytechnic Diploma courses).
  • Scholarship for all community students .
  • The wards of differently abled person holding the Identity card issued by the welfare Board of Differently Abled can apply for this Scholarship.

Document Required


  • Domicile Certificate
  • A Passport size photograph
  • caste certificate
  • Income declaration, a certificate from the parents/guardians
  • Mark sheet of previously qualified examination

How to Apply


  • Fresh Candidates : Whoever is a new candidate who has taken addmission this year The students who are studies in class 11 to PG level should apply in fresh.
  • Renewal Candidates : Candidates who are already enrolled in any school / college and had applied for scholarships last year will renew their application.
  • Kindly Collect the All Document Before Apply Online
  • If you are not now of the non refundable Amount, please check your fees receipt OR contact the college / school.
  • Submit your form to the concerned school / college within 3 days of the completion of the application.
  • For More Information Must Read the Full Instruction.
  • Aadhar Card is Compulsory for the Scholarship Application
  • Apply Offline through concerned District Office of Differently Abled Welfare.
